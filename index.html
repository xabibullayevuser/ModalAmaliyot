BMW M5 Competition Sport

BMW M Models

BMW M5 CS is a high-performance sedan that was produced by BMW in 2021. It is the most powerful M5 ever made, with a 635-horsepower twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The M5 CS can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.0 seconds and has a top speed of 200 mph (limited). BMW M5 CS was a limited-production model, with only 1,000 units produced worldwide. It has a starting price of $180,000.
